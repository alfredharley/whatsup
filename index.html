📌 Featured Topic This Week

"My boss keeps taking credit for my work — what should I do?"

Dr. Melee says: "Ah, the classic credit thief situation. In the Caribbean we have a saying: the coconut doesn't fall far from the tree. Your boss learned this somewhere. Here's what you need to do — first, start documenting everything..."

Full response from Dr. Melee:

First, start documenting everything immediately. Every email, every meeting, every project deliverable — keep a record with timestamps. This is your evidence trail.

Second, start CC'ing your boss's boss on important updates. Not in a sneaky way — just in a "keeping everyone informed" way. This puts your name on record.

Third, have a direct but professional conversation with your boss. Say: "I want to make sure I'm getting visibility for my contributions. How can we structure our work together so that happens?"

If none of this works — start updating your resume. Because a boss who steals credit doesn't get better. They get worse. You deserve better. 💪🏾
