The Opportunity

The Caribbean is Underserved by Technology

Millions of tourists and residents rely on fragmented, outdated tools to discover what to do, where to eat, and where to stay across the islands.

😤

No Single Discovery Platform

Locals and visitors juggle TripAdvisor, Google Maps, WhatsApp groups, and word of mouth just to find a restaurant or event.

📵

No Caribbean-First News Platform

Island news is scattered across Facebook pages and outdated websites. There is no modern, mobile-first Caribbean media platform.

🏨

Local Businesses Are Invisible

Local restaurants, guest houses, and event promoters have no affordable, unified platform to reach their customers.

🌍

Global Platforms Ignore the Caribbean

Airbnb, OpenTable, and Eventbrite exist but none are built for Caribbean culture, currency, or community.

What's Up? Solves All of This

One platform that answers the question every local and visitor asks every day — "What's up on this island?" — and connects them to every experience, business, and piece of news they need.

AI-powered local discovery and recommendations
Restaurant reservations and dining guides
Event listings and integrated ticket sales

Caribbean news, video, and live media

The Product

Nine Modules. One Platform.

What's Up? is not a directory — it is a Caribbean lifestyle operating system. Every module is designed around one question: what should I do next on this island?

🍽️

Where to Dine

Premium restaurant discovery with request-to-book reservations, menus, ratings, and curated dining guides by district.

Revenue: Commission
🎉

Events & Tickets

Local event listings with integrated ticket sales, WhatsApp purchase flow, and save-to-calendar functionality.

Revenue: Ticket Fees
🏠

Stay Local

Local accommodation marketplace featuring villas, guest houses, studios and apartments with smart lock integration roadmap.

Revenue: Booking Fee
🏷️

Deals & Coupons

QR-enabled local deals engine. Businesses submit offers, users redeem via QR code at the venue. Analytics included.

Revenue: Subscriptions
📰

News & Video

Location-aware Caribbean news with live YouTube integration, short-burst articles, and AI-assisted editorial workflow.

Revenue: Advertising
🌶️

Dr. Melee

Anonymous Caribbean community platform with Island Talk commentary and Ask Dr. Melee advice column. AI-moderated.

Revenue: Engagement
✨

AI Concierge

Location and time-aware AI that recommends what to do next. Suggests restaurants, events, and experiences based on the user's island, time, and preferences.

Feature: Retention
💼

Business Dashboard

Every restaurant, host, and event promoter gets a self-service dashboard to manage listings, bookings, photos, and analytics.

Revenue: Subscriptions
🛡️

Admin Platform

Full role-based admin system for multi-island management, content approval, sponsor management, and real-time analytics.

Feature: Operations
