The Coupon Engine

Run Promotions Across Every Bar
and Restaurant on SXM — Instantly

What's Up? gives ILTT a direct-to-consumer promotional engine. Create a deal, set the terms, and every user on the platform can find, save, and redeem it at your partner venues — with full tracking.

🍺

ILTT Creates the Offer

Set the product, discount, participating venues, validity dates, and redemption limit via the admin dashboard.

📲

What's Up? Pushes It

The offer appears in the Deals section, on the home screen, and as a push notification to all relevant users.

📱

Consumer Taps & Saves

User taps the deal, saves it to their profile, and sees the QR code or promo code to use at the venue.

✅

Venue Scans & Redeems

Bartender or server scans the QR code. Redemption is logged. ILTT sees exactly how many units moved per venue.
